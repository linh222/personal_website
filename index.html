Research Area: AI, NLP, Lifelogging, Multimodal Learning

Biography

I received my BSc in Data Science from University of Information Technology, Vietnam National University in Ho Chi Minh City. I am doing my PhD in ADAPT Centre, School of Computing, Dublin City University under supervision of Prof Cathal Gurrin and Prof Gareth Jones. My research interests lie in question-qnswering in lifelog, personal assistant and chatbot. I have two-year working experience in data analysis, and data engineering. I have been involved in projects related to customer reviews analysis, users and lead scoring, finance prediction, and lifelog retrieval. I aim to develop intelligent assistants that enhance human memory and interaction with digital information.
